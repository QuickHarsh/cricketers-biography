Personal Life and Off-Field Contributions

Beyond cricket, Virat Kohli’s personal life and off-field contributions reflect his multifaceted personality and deep sense of responsibility. He married Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma in December 2017, forming one of India’s most beloved power couples. Together, they welcomed their daughter, Vamika, in January 2021. Kohli often shares glimpses of his personal life, showcasing his love for family and his grounded nature despite his global fame. Kohli is not just a sports icon but also a philanthropist and entrepreneur. Through the Virat Kohli Foundation (VKF), he supports underprivileged children, promotes sports at the grassroots level, and provides assistance to emerging athletes. His foundation collaborates with various NGOs to work on education, healthcare, and disaster relief. He has also been actively involved in animal welfare campaigns, advocating for the ethical treatment of animals. On the business front, Kohli has ventured into several entrepreneurial pursuits. He owns One8, a lifestyle and fitness brand, and has invested in various startups, including fitness centers and a football club in India. As a global ambassador for leading brands, he uses his influence to promote sustainability and healthy living. Kohli’s off-field contributions, combined with his immense popularity, make him a role model for millions. His ability to balance personal life, philanthropic endeavors, and a high-profile cricketing career is a testament to his character and determination. Through his actions, Virat Kohli continues to inspire not only as a cricketer but also as a compassionate and driven individual.
