THE RO-HITMAN SHARMA

Rohit Sharma, fondly known as the "Hitman" of Indian cricket, is one of the most accomplished and elegant batsmen in the history of the game. Born on April 30, 1987, in Nagpur, Maharashtra, Rohit is renowned for his impeccable timing, effortless six-hitting ability, and match-winning performances. Over the years, he has established himself as a key pillar of Indian cricket, excelling in all formats of the game. Rohit holds numerous records, including the highest individual score in ODIs (264) and three double centuries in the format, a feat unmatched by any cricketer. He is also the captain of the Mumbai Indians (MI) in the Indian Premier League (IPL), leading them to an unprecedented five titles, making him the most successful captain in IPL history. Known for his calm demeanor and strategic acumen, Rohit Sharma has become an indispensable leader for Team India. Off the field, he is a wildlife conservation advocate and a family-oriented individual who inspires millions with his journey from humble beginnings to global stardom.
