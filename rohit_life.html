Personal Life and Off-Field Contributions

Rohit Sharma married Ritika Sajdeh, his long-time friend and sports manager, in December 2015. The couple welcomed their daughter, Samaira, in December 2018. Rohit often shares moments from his personal life, showcasing his role as a loving husband and father. Off the field, Rohit is an active advocate for wildlife conservation. As a passionate animal lover, he has been associated with initiatives to protect endangered species, particularly rhinos and marine life. He is also involved in various charitable activities, including supporting underprivileged children through educational programs. Rohit has ventured into business with investments in sports and lifestyle brands. As one of the most marketable athletes, he endorses several global and Indian brands. His calm and composed personality, both on and off the field, makes him a role model for aspiring cricketers and fans worldwide.
